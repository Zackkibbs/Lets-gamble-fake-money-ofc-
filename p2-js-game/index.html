The object of the game is to get a score of 9 or closest to it. Players may play with the Bank, or against it (as Player), and/or place Tie bets at anytime. Ace counts as 1 while picture cards and tens as zero. Cards from 2 to 9 follow their face value. Each hand's final score is evaluated as the sum of the cards' point values less all multiples of ten. The hand that scores closer to 9 wins. Below is the hitting rules.
